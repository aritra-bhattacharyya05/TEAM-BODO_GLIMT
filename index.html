Quick Start

Image Authentication

Upload an image via drag-and-drop, file picker, clipboard paste, or URL. Veridex will analyze:

  • EXIF Metadata ‚Äî camera make/model, GPS, timestamps
  • Pixel Entropy ‚Äî statistical uniformity indicating AI upscaling
  • Noise Patterns ‚Äî sensor noise vs. synthetic smoothing
  • Edge Coherence ‚Äî natural edge transitions vs. AI blending
  • Compression Artifacts ‚Äî JPEG block patterns consistent with re-encoding

A 0‚Äì100 Authenticity Score is returned: 75+ indicates likely authentic, 45‚Äì74 uncertain, below 45 likely AI-generated or manipulated.

Text AI Detection

Paste any text ‚Äî articles, essays, emails, social posts ‚Äî to get a sentence-level breakdown. Each sentence is classified as AI Generated, Human Written, or Uncertain with confidence scores.

Supported Formats

Images: JPG, PNG, WebP up to 10 MB, or any public image URL. Text: plain text up to ~10,000 words.

API & Rate Limits

Veridex uses the LLaMA 3.3 70B model via Groq. Groq's free tier allows approximately 30 requests/minute. For higher throughput, upgrade your Groq plan at console.groq.com.

Privacy

Your API key lives only in sessionStorage ‚Äî never sent to our servers. Images and text are sent directly to Groq and not retained. See our for details.

Accuracy Notes

AI detection is probabilistic. A high AI score means patterns consistent with AI generation were found ‚Äî it is not proof of AI authorship. Use results as a signal, not a verdict. Short texts (< 50 words) may produce less reliable results.
